Level Up

Your character gains new heroic capabilities.

❤️
Increase Maximum Hit Points: You get one new Hit Die. You can either roll it and add your Constitution modifier, or take the fixed average (the number in parentheses in your class description) plus your Constitution modifier. This total is permanently added to your hit point maximum.
⚔️
Unlock Class Features: Look at your character's specific class table. You gain any new features, sub-class options, or extra attacks listed for your new level.
📜
Update Script Slots & Spells (For Scriptcasters): If you play a scriptcaster, you will gain access to more script slots and often get to learn or prepare new spells.
⭐
Boost Ability Scores or Feats: At certain levels (usually levels 4, 8, 12, 16, and 19), you get an Ability Score Improvement (ASI). You can add +2 to one ability score, +1 to two different ability scores, or choose a powerful Feat instead.
Available Now!
🎯
Adjust Proficiency Bonus: This bonus scales at levels 5, 9, 13, and 17. It affects how well you perform skills you are trained in, your weapon attack rolls, and your scriptcasting DCs.
