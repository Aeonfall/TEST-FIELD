Opportunity Attack

If a combatant moves away from an engaged enemy, it triggers an Opportunity Attack.

In 5e rules, this is a Reaction used to make one melee attack against a visible enemy that willingly moves out of your reach.

  • The attack occurs right before the target leaves your range.
  • You cannot use this if the enemy uses the Disengage action.
  • You cannot use this if the enemy is moved involuntarily.

Ready an Action

  • Reaction Cost: Holding an action uses your Reaction for this round.
  • Concentration: Readying a spell requires concentration until released.
  • Expiration: If the trigger doesn't occur before your next turn, the action is lost.

Animal Handling

1. Claiming / Befriending

When you first try to make an animal your companion, roll Animal Handling (1d20 + Modifier)

  • Success | Animal accepts you
  • Fail by 1–4 | Distrusts you (try later)
  • Fail by 5+ | Flees or attacks

2. Bonding Period

  • Spend 3–7 in-game days caring for the animal.
  • Make 1 Animal Handling check per day.
  • 3 successes = bonded companion.
  • Failure slows progress but does not break the bond.

3. Training the Pet

Each command requires training (1 check per day). 2 successes = command learned.
